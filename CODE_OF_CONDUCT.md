## Code of conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (latest version)**.
The official reference is available at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/

## 1. Pledge

We are committed to building a professional, respectful, diverse, and inclusive
working environment.

## 2. Encouraged behavior

- Respect opinions and differences.
- Communicate clearly and transparently.
- Provide support and share knowledge.

## 3. Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Actions that compromise safety or privacy.
- Posting inappropriate or unlawful content.

## 4. Reporting violations

Please contact: license@xnetvn.net

## 5. Enforcement

Violations will be handled according to the project's internal policy and may result
in restricted or terminated contribution rights.
