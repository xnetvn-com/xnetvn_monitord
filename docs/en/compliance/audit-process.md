## Audit process

## 1. Objective

Ensure changes, configuration, and logs meet internal audit requirements.

## 2. Scope

- Daemon configuration.
- Operational logs.
- Deployment and rollback processes.

## 3. Process

1. Collect relevant logs and configuration.
2. Compare against security and operations policies.
3. Report findings and recommendations.
