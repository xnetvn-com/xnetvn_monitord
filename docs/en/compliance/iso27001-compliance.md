## ISO 27001 compliance

## 1. Objective

Ensure information security controls for daemon configuration, logs, and operations.

## 2. Minimum controls

- Access management (least privilege).
- Log management and audit trails.
- Configuration change control.

## 3. Recommendations

- Apply change management for configuration.
- Periodically review access rights and logs.
