## Incident response policy

## 1. Objective

Detect, isolate, mitigate, and learn from system incidents.

## 2. Severity classification

- **P0**: Service outage or severe data loss.
- **P1**: Major functionality impact.
- **P2**: Partial impact with workarounds.
- **P3**: Minor issues with no operational impact.

## 3. Handling process

1. Detect and confirm the incident.
2. Isolate the root cause.
3. Fix and restore the service.
4. Report and perform RCA (Root Cause Analysis).

## 4. Reporting

- Record the timeline, impact scope, and mitigation actions.
- Preserve logs for audit.
